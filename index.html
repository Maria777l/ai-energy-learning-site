CALCULATION LAB

Turn capacity into annual electricity.

A 1 GW data centre running at a 90% load factor uses about 7.9 TWh per year before considering how its demand changes over time.

Energy = Power × Time × Load factor 1 GW × 8,760 h × 0.90 = 7.884 TWh Rule of thumb: 1 GW running continuously for one year = 8.76 TWh.
